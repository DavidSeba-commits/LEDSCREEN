Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LED displays

What is an LED screen and how does it work?

An LED screen (Light Emitting Diode screen) is a digital display that uses thousands or millions of tiny LEDs to produce images and video. Each LED acts as a pixel, emitting light in red, green, or blue. By combining these colors at varying intensities, LED screens can create millions of different colors and display any image or video content.

LED screens work by sending electrical signals to each LED, controlling their brightness and color. Modern LED displays use sophisticated controllers that manage millions of LEDs simultaneously to create smooth, high-quality visuals.

What are the advantages of LED displays over traditional displays?

LED displays offer numerous advantages including: superior brightness (visible even in direct sunlight), exceptional energy efficiency (up to 50% less power consumption), longer lifespan (100,000+ hours), better color accuracy, wider viewing angles, seamless scalability to any size, and weather resistance for outdoor applications.

Additionally, LED screens produce less heat, require minimal maintenance, and can be easily customized to fit any space or shape.

How long do LED screens last?

Our LED screens are designed for longevity, with an average lifespan of 100,000 hours or more. This translates to approximately 11 years of continuous operation or 20+ years with typical daily usage.

The actual lifespan depends on usage patterns, environmental conditions, and maintenance. Our displays feature intelligent power management that extends LED life by adjusting brightness based on ambient conditions.

Are LED screens energy efficient?

Yes! LED displays are highly energy efficient. Our screens consume up to 50% less energy compared to traditional display technologies. They use intelligent brightness control, automatically adjusting power consumption based on ambient light conditions.

For example, our typical outdoor LED screen consumes approximately 150-300W per square meter, while indoor displays use even less. This translates to significant energy savings over the display's lifetime.

Can LED screens work in outdoor conditions?

Absolutely! Our outdoor LED screens are specifically engineered for harsh environmental conditions. They feature IP65 or higher protection ratings, making them dust-proof and water-resistant.

Outdoor displays operate reliably in temperatures ranging from -20°C to 60°C, with high brightness levels (5000-8000 nits) that ensure visibility even in direct sunlight. They're also built to withstand wind, rain, and other weather conditions.

What resolution should I choose for my LED display?

Resolution choice depends on viewing distance and application. For close-up viewing (retail, corporate environments), we recommend higher pixel density with smaller pixel pitch (P1.2-P2.5). For medium distances (events, conferences), P2.5-P5 works well. For outdoor billboards viewed from far away, P6-P10 is typically sufficient.

We offer resolutions from HD to 8K. Our team can help you determine the optimal resolution based on your specific needs and budget.

How much does an LED screen cost?

LED screen costs vary based on size, resolution, indoor/outdoor rating, and features. Generally, prices range from a few thousand dollars for small indoor displays to hundreds of thousands for large outdoor installations.

Factors affecting cost include: pixel pitch (higher resolution = higher cost), brightness requirements, weather protection, installation complexity, and additional features like transparency or flexibility. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer installation and maintenance services?

Yes! We provide comprehensive installation services performed by certified technicians. This includes site assessment, structural engineering, installation, calibration, and training for your team.

We also offer ongoing maintenance packages including 24/7 technical support, regular inspections, cleaning, software updates, and prompt repairs. Our preventive maintenance programs help maximize your display's lifespan and performance.

Can LED screens be customized to any size or shape?

Yes! One of the greatest advantages of LED technology is its modular design. Screens can be built to virtually any size or shape by combining individual LED panels or modules.

We can create curved displays, circular screens, irregular shapes, and even 3D structures. Our flexible LED panels can bend up to 360°, enabling creative installations that were previously impossible. Share your vision with us, and we'll make it reality.

What content can I display on LED screens?

LED screens can display virtually any digital content including: videos (all formats), images, live feeds, social media content, real-time data, interactive content, 3D animations, and more.

Our displays support standard video formats (MP4, AVI, MOV) and image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF). We provide user-friendly content management systems that allow you to schedule, update, and manage content remotely from any device.

Absolutely! We offer flexible rental solutions for events, conferences, concerts, trade shows, and temporary installations. Our rental service includes delivery, installation, technical support, and pickup.

Rental packages are available for durations from single days to several months. All rental equipment is professionally maintained and includes backup units to ensure your event runs smoothly.

Extended warranty options are available for up to 5 years. All warranties cover manufacturing defects and hardware failures. We also provide lifetime technical support for all products.
