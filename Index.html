What Are LED Screens?

An LED screen—short for Light Emitting Diode screen—is a type of digital display that uses thousands (or even millions) of tiny LEDs to produce images and video.

LED screens are everywhere: from Times Square billboards to sleek retail signage, concert backdrops, and even architectural art. Their brightness allows them to be used outdoors where they are visible in the sun for store signs and billboards.
