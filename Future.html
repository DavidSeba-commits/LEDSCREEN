Our Innovation Roadmap

Charting the course of LED display evolution

2025

MicroLED Mass Production

Scaling up production of ultra-efficient MicroLED displays with 0.5mm pixel pitch for unprecedented image quality and energy savings.

2026

AI Content Optimization

Launch of next-generation AI systems that automatically enhance content, predict maintenance needs, and optimize energy consumption in real-time.

2027

Holographic LED Displays

Introduction of 3D holographic LED screens that create floating images without glasses, revolutionizing retail and entertainment experiences.

2028

Quantum Dot Integration

Combining quantum dot technology with LEDs to achieve 100% color gamut coverage and colors beyond current display capabilities.

2030

Zero-Carbon Displays

Achieving complete carbon neutrality with solar-powered displays, recyclable materials, and zero-emission manufacturing processes.

Breakthrough Technologies We're Developing

The next generation of visual experiences

Neural Display Interface

Displays that respond to brain signals and eye movements, creating personalized viewing experiences and accessibility for users with disabilities.

Volumetric 3D Displays

True 3D visualization without glasses, allowing viewers to see objects from any angle with depth and parallax in free space.

Touchless Interaction

Gesture and voice-controlled displays using advanced sensors and AI, enabling hygienic, intuitive interaction from a distance.

Energy Harvesting

Self-powered displays that generate electricity from ambient light, movement, and electromagnetic fields, eliminating power cables.

Nano-Pixel Technology

Displays with pixel sizes measured in nanometers, achieving resolutions beyond 16K for medical imaging and scientific applications.

Atmospheric Projection

LED systems that project images onto air molecules, creating displays in open spaces without physical screens.
